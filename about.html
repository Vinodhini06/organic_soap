About Us

Discover the story behind our handcrafted organic soaps and the values that drive our mission.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Organic Soap began as a small kitchen experiment by our founder, Emma Green, who was searching for natural skincare solutions for her sensitive skin.

What started as a personal quest quickly blossomed into a passion for creating pure, organic soaps that nurture both the skin and the planet. Emma's background in herbalism and aromatherapy informed her early recipes, which quickly gained popularity among friends and family.

By 2015, Organic Soap had grown from a home-based business to a dedicated workshop with a small team of artisans who shared Emma's vision for sustainable, ethical skincare. Today, we continue to handcraft each soap in small batches, ensuring the highest quality and maintaining the personal touch that has been our hallmark since day one.

Our journey has been guided by a simple philosophy: what you put on your body is just as important as what you put in it. This belief drives everything we do, from sourcing ingredients to designing our eco-friendly packaging.

Our Mission & Values

We're committed to creating exceptional skincare products that honor both people and planet through these core principles:

Sustainability

We source ingredients responsibly, use minimal and recyclable packaging, and continuously work to reduce our environmental footprint.

Purity

We use only natural, organic ingredients with no synthetic fragrances, preservatives, or harsh chemicals that can harm your skin or the environment.

Ethical Sourcing

We partner with fair trade suppliers and local farmers whenever possible, ensuring fair compensation and sustainable practices throughout our supply chain.

Compassion

We never test on animals and donate a portion of our profits to environmental conservation and community wellness initiatives.

Our Artisanal Process

Each bar of Organic Soap is handcrafted with care through our meticulous small-batch process:

1

Thoughtful Formulation

We carefully design each recipe to address specific skin needs while ensuring a luxurious experience. Our formulations balance cleansing, moisturizing, and therapeutic properties.

2

Sourcing & Preparation

We source the finest organic oils, butters, botanicals, and essential oils. Each ingredient is carefully measured and prepared to ensure optimal quality and performance.

3

Cold Process Method

We use the traditional cold process method, which preserves the natural glycerin and beneficial properties of our ingredients. This time-honored technique creates a gentle, moisturizing soap.

4

Curing & Aging

After pouring, our soaps cure for 4-6 weeks in a temperature-controlled environment. This patient aging process creates a harder, milder bar with a rich lather and long-lasting quality.

5

Cutting & Trimming

Each soap loaf is hand-cut into individual bars, then carefully trimmed and beveled by our artisans. This attention to detail ensures a beautiful, consistent product.

6

Packaging & Delivery

We wrap each soap in eco-friendly, biodegradable packaging with minimal environmental impact. Your soap is then carefully packed and shipped to arrive in perfect condition.

Why We Are Best in the Industry

Our commitment to excellence sets us apart in the natural skincare market:

Award-Winning Formulations

Our soaps have won multiple industry awards for quality, innovation, and sustainability. Our signature Lavender Dreams formula was named "Best Natural Soap" by Green Living Magazine three years running.

Triple-Certified Organic

We hold certifications from USDA Organic, COSMOS Organic, and EcoCert, ensuring our ingredients meet the highest standards of organic purity and sustainable production practices.

Innovative Research

Our dedicated R&D team continuously researches new botanical ingredients and formulation techniques. We collaborate with dermatologists to ensure our products deliver real skin benefits.

Master Artisans

Our soap makers undergo a rigorous two-year apprenticeship program before becoming certified artisans. This ensures exceptional craftsmanship and consistency in every batch we produce.

Zero-Waste Commitment

We operate a zero-waste production facility where all byproducts are reused, recycled, or composted. Our packaging is 100% plastic-free and made from post-consumer recycled materials.

Community Impact

Through our Soap for Hope program, we donate one bar of soap to shelters and community organizations for every ten bars sold. We've provided over 100,000 bars to those in need since 2015.

Our Workshop & Facility

Located in the heart of Green Valley, our solar-powered workshop is where the magic happens. We've designed our production facility to minimize environmental impact while maximizing craftsmanship and quality.

Our 5,000 square foot space houses our soap production area, curing room, packaging station, and research laboratory. Large windows provide natural light throughout the workspace, reducing energy consumption while creating an inspiring environment for our artisans.

We harvest rainwater for use in our facility, compost all organic waste, and use energy-efficient equipment throughout our operations. Our workshop is open for public tours every Saturday, where visitors can see our soap-making process firsthand and learn about sustainable manufacturing practices.
