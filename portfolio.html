Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

Founded in a small kitchen with a passion for natural skincare and environmental sustainability.

2012

First Retail Store

Opened our first brick-and-mortar store in Green Valley, California, showcasing our handcrafted soaps to the local community.

2015

Organic Certification

Achieved USDA Organic certification for all our products, confirming our commitment to using only the purest ingredients.

2018

National Expansion

Expanded our product line and distribution to reach customers nationwide through partnerships with premium retailers.

2020

Sustainable Packaging Initiative

Launched our zero-waste packaging initiative, eliminating plastic from our entire product line and introducing compostable alternatives.

2023

Global Presence

Expanded to international markets with a focus on Europe and Asia, bringing our organic soaps to conscious consumers worldwide.
